Make the Move!

North Carolina is Waiting!

Where are we?

The beautiful colony of North Carolina is one of the Southern Colonies located directly south of Virginia.

What does our land look like?

Our colony has three main geographic regions. These are the Coastal region in the east, the Piedmont region in the midde, and the Appalacian region in the far west.

Our Epic Economy!

North Carolina is proud to export rich tobacco and soft cotton from our colony. There is plenty of area for settlers to start a farm and use laborers to earn a pretty penny.

In addition to these two crops, the local Indians were kind and introduced us to many new delicious crops like corn, sweet potatoes, beans, squash, pumpkins, and peanuts!

Have a voice!

We pride ourself in allowing our citizens to have a voice in what happens in our colony. That is exactly why we have our representatives elected by popular vote[1]!

Our House of Burgesses was established just for this purpose. It ensures that everyone is represented.

Relations with Locals!

We have established a relation with the local peoples of North Carolina. As stated previously, they were kind enough to intricude us to many new crops.

we believe that us colonists and the native Indians can live in harmony in North Carolina.
